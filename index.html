This is a page where I'll upload presentations and information about the NMR Club at NTNU. The meetings are organized once a month. The meeting schedule will be posted here.

Purpose of the NMR Club

There are many NMR users at NTNU, working with different aspects and applications of MMR. This is meant to be a meeting place to present, discuss and do calculations of various concepts of NMR spectroscopy.
