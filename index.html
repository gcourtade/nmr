NMR Club

This page is meant as a repository for presentations and information about the NMR Club at NTNU. The meetings are organized once a month and the meeting schedule will be posted here.

Contact Gaston Courtade if you’d like to know more and to be notified about upcoming meetings.

Purpose of the NMR Club

There are many NMR users at NTNU, working with different aspects and applications of NMR. This is meant to be a meeting place to present, discuss and do calculations of various concepts of NMR spectroscopy.

It is meant to be a low-threshold meeting place, where we can share knowledge and learn from each other. In other words, you don’t have to know a lot about NMR to join if you are curious about it.
